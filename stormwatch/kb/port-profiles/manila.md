# Port Profile: Manila (PHMNL)

- Code: PHMNL
- Location: 14.58°N, 120.97°E
- Country: Philippines
- Weather Vulnerability Score: 0.85

## Key Facts
- Located directly in the Western Pacific typhoon corridor
- Experiences 12–15 typhoons within 300km per year
- Highest weather vulnerability of any major Asian container port
- Handles 5.5M TEU/year — critical transshipment hub for Southeast Asia

## Weather Risks
- Typhoon exposure: Direct hit from Category 3+ typhoon every 2–3 years
- Storm surge risk: Manila Bay amplifies surge by 1.5–2x
- Port closure: 3–5 days for Category 3+, 5–7 days for Category 4+
- Secondary impact: road flooding cuts port access for additional 1–3 days

## Mitigation Options
- Singapore alternate (SGSIN): Primary diversion port, adds 4–5 days
- Hong Kong alternate (HKHKG): Closer but also typhoon-exposed
- Ships with >48h lead time: 80% successfully reroute and avoid delays
- Ships with <12h warning: nearly 100% experience significant delays
