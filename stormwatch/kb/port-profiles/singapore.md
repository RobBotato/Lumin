# Port Profile: Singapore (SGSIN)

- Code: SGSIN
- Location: 1.26°N, 103.84°E
- Country: Singapore
- Weather Vulnerability Score: 0.30

## Key Facts
- 2nd busiest container port globally (37M TEU/year)
- Located near equator: outside typhoon belt, minimal tropical cyclone risk
- Primary alternate port for South China Sea disruptions
- Strategic position at Strait of Malacca entrance

## Weather Risks
- Typhoon risk: Near zero (equatorial location)
- Monsoon rain: November–January, minimal operational impact
- Lightning risk: Frequent thunderstorms cause intermittent crane stoppages (2–4 hours)
- Haze from regional fires: September–October, visibility restrictions possible

## Mitigation Options
- Serves as THE primary reroute destination for South China Sea typhoons
- Rerouting from Manila/Hong Kong to Singapore adds 4–5 days transit
- Port capacity: Can absorb 15–20% additional volume during regional disruptions
- Pre-positioning strategy: Cargo staged at Singapore reduces Asia–Europe impact by 25%
