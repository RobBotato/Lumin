# Port Profile: Los Angeles (USLAX)

- Code: USLAX
- Location: 33.74°N, -118.27°W
- Country: United States
- Weather Vulnerability Score: 0.25

## Key Facts
- Busiest US container port (10M TEU/year at LA/LB complex)
- Primary receiving port for all trans-Pacific cargo
- Low storm risk: Mediterranean climate, rare severe weather
- Acts as receiving port for diverted Atlantic/Gulf cargo during hurricanes

## Weather Risks
- Storm risk: Low. Pacific storms rarely reach Southern California with severity
- Fog risk: May–July morning marine layer reduces visibility, minimal delays
- Earthquake risk: Seismic activity could impact port infrastructure (rare)
- Secondary risk: Overwhelmed during Gulf/Atlantic hurricane diversions

## Mitigation Options
- Primary diversion destination for Houston cargo during Gulf hurricanes
- LA/LB complex can absorb 10–15% additional volume before congestion
- Long Beach adjacent port provides overflow capacity
