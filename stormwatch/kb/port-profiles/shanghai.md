# Port Profile: Shanghai (CNSHA)

- Code: CNSHA
- Location: 31.23°N, 121.47°E
- Country: China
- Weather Vulnerability Score: 0.65

## Key Facts
- World's busiest container port (47M TEU/year)
- Exposed to typhoons July–October (average 4–6 typhoons within 500km annually)
- Port closure triggers cascade effect to entire US West Coast supply chain
- 72% of trans-Pacific container traffic originates from or transits through Shanghai

## Weather Risks
- Typhoon exposure: Category 2+ storms within 300km occur 2–3 times per year
- Fog risk: November–March, reduces port efficiency by 15%
- Storm surge: Yangtze estuary vulnerable to 1–2m surge during typhoons

## Mitigation Options
- Ningbo alternate port (29.87°N, 121.55°E): 150km south, similar capacity
- Vessels with >72h warning can reroute to Ningbo with <1 day additional delay
- Cargo pre-positioning at Los Angeles reduces downstream Shanghai closure impact by 30%
